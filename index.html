Introduction

Since the first agricultural revolution, we have tamed and domesticated animals for use in farming and herding. Now we have pets that are our companions, and whom we cherish. Here are some wonderful examples of pets.

Cats

Cats love fish, lasers, and scratching stuff. They don't shed as much hair as dogs, and they clean themselves.

Dogs

Dogs love bones, tennis balls, and sticks. Most dogs shed tons of hair, and are hard to clean.

Reptiles

There are many kinds of reptiles. Snakes, lizards, turtles, and others. They don't have hair, and are easy to clean.
